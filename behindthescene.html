Behind the scene

01

The Idea

I am a football fan and I go to football matches almost every weekend. Football unites people. Although we may never ever seen someone in our day-to-day life, when we at the football game, cheering and shouting for the same, we are a big family.

However, it's not always happy memories. I've experienced few racial incidents myself and it broke my heart. No matter if we support different teams, nor we are different races, football is the beautiful game.

There should be no place for racism in football, neither in the society! As a victim, I wanted to speak up for myself and also for others who had similar experience...

03

The Animation

I am a illustrator and graphic designer, but I have never did any animation before. This was a big challenge for me.

I decided to go with a sketchy, old-school animation style, which means most of times I drew individual frames and then stitched them together in the video editing software, almost like a flipbook.

It was challenging but at the same time, it was enjoyable!
